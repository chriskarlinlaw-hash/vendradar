## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording Setup
- **Screen recording:** Use Loom, QuickTime, or OBS (1080p minimum)
- **Voiceover:** Clear audio (use a decent mic or iPhone headphones in a quiet room)
- **Talking head (optional):** Record intro/outro on iPhone or webcam
- **Editing:** CapCut (free), iMovie, or Descript ($12/month)

### Visuals to Prepare
- VendRadar dashboard (use demo data or real data)
- Heat map examples (make sure they're visually clear)
- Side-by-side comparison (gym vs office)
- ROI calculator screenshot

### Hook Variations (A/B Test)
Test different hooks on short-form (TikTok/Reels) to see what gets most views:

1. "What if I told you that vending machine could make $500/month instead of $50?"
2. "Most vending machines fail because of one mistake. Here's how to avoid it."
3. "I lost $2,000 placing vending machines in bad locations. Here's what I built to fix it."

### Music (Optional)
- Upbeat background music at low volume (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library)
- Avoid anything too distracting â€” the data is the star

### Captions
- Add captions (auto-generate in YouTube Studio or Descript)
- 85% of social videos are watched without sound
